Five weeks. Watch in order if you can. Each session has a video, the key teachings, and a link to download the documents that go with it.

Week 01

Credit Foundations — What's Actually On Your Report

We start at the foundation. What a credit report actually shows you, how the bureaus structure the data, and the sections most people never read carefully enough. Before you can fix anything, you have to understand what you're looking at.

Key Teachings

  • The four major sections of every credit report and what each one means
  • Why your personal info section is the foundation for every dispute
  • How to spot errors most consumers walk past
  • The three bureaus and why their reports look different
  • Where to pull your real reports for free

Week 02

Understanding Your Credit Score — The Models That Score You

Now that you know what's on your report, let's talk about how it gets scored. Different models score differently. The number you see on your phone app is rarely the number a lender pulls. Here's how to know which score actually matters for your situation.

Key Teachings

  • The five factors that make up your FICO score and how each one is weighted
  • FICO 8 vs FICO 10T vs VantageScore 4.0 — when each one shows up
  • Why mortgage lenders use older models (FICO 2, 4, and 5)
  • Utilization, the most controllable factor, explained right
  • What hurts your score the most and what you can fix fastest
Get Week 2 Documents (PDF) ↓
Week 03

The DIY Dispute Process — Your Federal Rights In Action

The dispute process is your federal right under the Fair Credit Reporting Act. You don't need a credit repair company. You need to know the order, the timing, and the leverage points. This week we walk through the actual process from Round 1 through escalation.

Key Teachings

  • Why personal information cleanup comes before any account dispute
  • The Round 1 letter — what to include and what NOT to include
  • How to read bureau response letters and what each one actually means
  • The Method of Verification demand and why it's the bridge to deletion
  • Certified mail rules and the paper trail that protects you
Get Week 3 Documents (PDF) ↓
Week 04

Building Credit That Lasts — The Profile That Actually Works

Disputing alone won't move you forward. The build is the other half of the work. This week we cover the credit profile that actually works — the right number of accounts, the right mix, the right utilization. The blueprint Cousins use to get to 750 and stay there.

Key Teachings

  • Four open primary accounts — why this is the sweet spot
  • The two credit cards that anchor your profile
  • Short-term and long-term installments and why both matter
  • Credit-builder loans for thin files or rebuilders
  • Utilization under 10 percent — the discipline that compounds
Get Week 4 Documents (PDF) ↓
Week 05

Putting It All Together — Your 12-Month Plan

The final week. We pull everything together into a 12-month plan you can actually follow. Disputes running, build compounding, monitoring in place. The Cousins who win in a year are the ones who put the pieces in the right order. Here's how.

Key Teachings

  • Sequencing the dispute process and the build at the same time
  • What to do every month for the next 12 months
  • How to know when to escalate vs when to wait
  • Tracking your wins and adjusting when something stalls
  • What comes after — mortgage readiness, business credit, the next chapter
