Yes. The page is written for people who need credit basics as well as people who are ready for a more strategic next step.

You will enter the Credit Academy member flow where you can access the portal, connect required monitoring when prompted, and begin using the available education and tools.

Can current members use this page?

Yes. Current members can use the login button to return to the member portal without creating a new account.
